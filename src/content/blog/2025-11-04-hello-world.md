I left this site alone for a while, which turned out to be useful.

Coming back to it, I can see more clearly what it should become: not a generic blog, not a rigid portfolio, but a place where writing and travel imagery can sit next to each other without competing.

This rebuild starts there, with a quieter structure and a more deliberate rhythm.
