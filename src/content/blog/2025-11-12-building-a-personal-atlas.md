I want this site to behave less like a storage bucket and more like a personal atlas.

That means each page should do a little more than file content away. It should suggest a pace. A relation between text and image. A sense that entries belong to an ongoing body of travel, memory, and attention rather than separate isolated uploads.

The best portfolio sites I return to never feel crowded. They feel edited. They make room for sequence, for silence, and for the possibility that one image can hold the whole page for a second before you move on.
